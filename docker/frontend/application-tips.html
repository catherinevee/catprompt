Application Status Definitions
Submitted

Your application has been received and is in the queue for initial processing.

Under Review

Housing staff are reviewing your application and verifying all requirements have been met.

Approved

Your application has been approved and you're eligible for housing assignment based on your priority date.

Waitlisted

Housing is currently full, but you're on the waitlist and will be notified if space becomes available.

Incomplete

Additional information or documentation is required before your application can be processed.

Assigned

You have been assigned housing and will receive detailed information about your room and move-in process.

Common Questions

How long does the review process take?

Most complete applications are reviewed within 2-3 weeks of submission. During peak periods (February-April), processing may take up to 4 weeks.

What if my status shows "Incomplete"?

Check your email for a message from the Housing Office detailing what's missing. You can also call (555) 123-4567 for specific information about your application.

When will I find out my room assignment?

Room assignments are made based on your application priority date. You'll receive an email when your assignment is ready, typically 2-4 weeks before the semester begins.

Can I change my housing preferences after submitting?

Yes, you can update your preferences until your application moves to "Under Review" status. Contact the Housing Office to make changes.

What happens if I'm waitlisted?

You'll remain on the waitlist and be contacted if space becomes available. Continue to check your status regularly as situations can change quickly.

How do I know if all my documents were received?

Your application status page will show which requirements have been completed. Missing items will be clearly marked and you'll receive email reminders.
