Emergency Services

Access critical emergency resources, safety procedures, and 24/7 support services. Your safety and well-being are our highest priorities on campus.

LIFE-THREATENING EMERGENCY

For immediate medical, fire, or police response

911

Always call 911 first for life-threatening emergencies
Then contact Campus Safety for additional support

National Emergency Hotlines

Critical national emergency and crisis support resources available 24/7 across the United States.

Life-Threatening Emergency

911
Fire, medical emergency, violent crime, serious injury, or any life-threatening situation nationwide.
24/7 Response

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

988
24/7 free and confidential support for people in distress, crisis prevention resources, and mental health assistance.
24/7 Available

RAINN National Sexual Assault Hotline

1-800-656-HOPE (4673)
Confidential support from trained staff members for survivors of sexual assault and their loved ones.
24/7 Available

National Domestic Violence Hotline

1-800-799-SAFE (7233)
Confidential support, safety planning, and resources for those experiencing domestic violence.
24/7 Available

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741
Free, 24/7 crisis support via text message for anyone in crisis. Text-based counseling available.
24/7 Available

National Child Abuse Hotline

1-800-4-A-CHILD (1-800-422-4453)
Crisis intervention, professional counseling, and referrals to local services for child abuse cases.
24/7 Available

Specialized Crisis Support

LGBTQ National Hotline

1-888-843-4564
Confidential support, information, and local resources for LGBTQ individuals and their families.
Daily: 4PM-12AM EST

SAMHSA National Helpline

1-800-662-HELP (4357)
Substance abuse and mental health treatment referral and information service for individuals and families.
24/7 Available

National Eating Disorders Association

1-800-931-2237
Support, resources, and treatment referrals for individuals struggling with eating disorders.
Mon-Thu: 11AM-9PM EST
Fri: 11AM-5PM EST

National Human Trafficking Hotline

1-888-373-7888
24/7 confidential support for victims and survivors of human trafficking and the general public.
24/7 Available

online.rainn.org
Secure, anonymous online chat with trained support specialists from RAINN.
24/7 Available

Veterans Crisis Line

24/7 Available
