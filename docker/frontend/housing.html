Housing Services

Your comprehensive resource for campus housing. Apply for residence halls, manage your current housing, and access all housing-related services and information. Priority applications due March 15th. Late applications accepted through May 1st on a space-available basis.

Housing Application Portal

Ready to apply for campus housing? Our online application system makes it easy to submit your preferences and required documents.

Start New Application

Application Requirements

✓ Completed Housing Application Form

  • ☐ Personal and academic information (name, student ID, major, GPA)
  • ☐ Housing preferences and lifestyle questionnaire
  • ☐ Emergency contacts and application signature

✓ Housing Contract Agreement

  • ☐ Read and understand all terms and conditions
  • ☐ Review payment schedules and cancellation policies
  • ☐ Sign and submit contract before deadline

✓ $300 Housing Deposit (Refundable)

  • ☐ Complete online payment through student portal
  • ☐ Save payment confirmation and receipt
  • ☐ Verify deposit applied to housing account

✓ Immunization Records

  • ☐ MMR, Meningococcal, and COVID-19 vaccinations
  • ☐ TB screening (if required) and provider certification
  • ☐ Submit all records to Student Health Services

✓ Special Accommodation Requests (If Applicable)

  • ☐ Obtain medical documentation and contact Disability Services
  • ☐ Submit detailed accommodation requests 60 days early
  • ☐ Follow up on approval and coordinate implementation
