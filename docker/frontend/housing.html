Important Housing Deadlines

Fall 2024 Housing Applications: Priority deadline is March 15th. Late applications accepted through May 1st on a space-available basis.

Application Requirements

  • Completed housing application form
  • Housing contract agreement
  • $300 housing deposit (refundable)
  • Immunization records
  • Special accommodation requests (if applicable)
Housing Options

Explore our variety of housing options designed to meet different preferences and budgets.

Traditional Residence Halls

Double and triple occupancy rooms with shared bathrooms and common areas. Perfect for first-year students looking to build community.

$3,200 - $3,800 per semester

Suite-Style Living

Private bedrooms with shared living space and bathroom. Ideal for students who want more privacy while still having roommates.

$3,800 - $4,400 per semester

Apartment-Style Housing

Fully furnished apartments with kitchen, living room, and private bedrooms. Perfect for upperclassmen and graduate students.

$4,400 - $5,200 per semester

Special Interest Communities

Themed housing for students with shared interests, including honors, international, wellness, and academic learning communities.

$3,400 - $4,600 per semester
