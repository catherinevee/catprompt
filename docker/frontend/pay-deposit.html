Deposit Information

What is the Housing Deposit?

The housing deposit is a refundable security deposit that secures your place in the housing assignment process and covers potential damages or outstanding charges.

Deposit Details

  • Amount: $300.00 (same for all housing types)
  • Due Date: Within 10 days of application submission
  • Refundable: Yes, upon satisfactory checkout
  • Processing Fee: 2.75% for credit/debit card payments
  • Alternative Payment: Bank transfer available (no processing fee)

When You Get Your Deposit Back

  • Upon completion of your housing contract
  • When you properly check out of your assigned housing
  • After any damages or outstanding charges are deducted
  • If you cancel your housing before the deadline

Deposit May Be Used For

  • Room damage beyond normal wear and tear
  • Missing or damaged furniture/equipment
  • Cleaning fees for excessive mess
  • Unpaid housing or dining charges
  • Early termination fees (if applicable)
Frequently Asked Questions

What if I can't pay the full deposit right now?

The housing deposit must be paid in full to secure your housing priority. If you need financial assistance, contact the Financial Aid Office at (555) 123-4574 to discuss emergency aid options.

Can someone else pay my housing deposit?

Yes, parents, guardians, or other authorized individuals can pay your deposit. They will need your student ID number and personal information for verification.

What happens if I change my mind about on-campus housing?

You can cancel your housing application and receive a full refund of your deposit if you cancel before the published deadline (typically 30 days before the semester begins).

Is the deposit the same for all housing types?

Yes, the $300 deposit is the same whether you're applying for traditional dorms, suites, or apartments. The deposit amount does not vary by housing type or cost.

How long does it take to process my payment?

Credit and debit card payments are processed immediately. Bank transfers may take 2-3 business days to process. You'll receive email confirmation once payment is complete.

What if my payment is declined?

Contact your bank to ensure the card is authorized for online payments and has sufficient funds. You can also try a different payment method or contact our office for assistance.
