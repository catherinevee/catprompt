Frequently Asked Questions
How do I connect to the campus WiFi?
Select "Campus-Secure" from available networks, enter your student username and password, and accept the security certificate. For detailed instructions, visit our Student Services page.
How do I request a room change?
Log into the Housing Portal and submit a room change request. Changes are processed based on availability and must be approved by Residence Life staff. There may be associated fees.
Can I change my meal plan during the semester?
Meal plan changes are allowed during the first two weeks of each semester. After this period, changes are only permitted for documented medical or dietary needs.
What if I lose my student ID card?
Report lost cards immediately to deactivate them and prevent unauthorized use. Visit the Student ID Office to get a replacement card for a $15 fee. Bring a government-issued photo ID.
How do I reset my password?
Use the password reset tool on the login page or contact the IT Help Desk. You'll need your student ID number and access to your recovery email or phone number.
