WiFi Setup Guide

Connect to the campus WiFi network in just a few simple steps. Choose your device type below for specific instructions.

Windows Setup Mac Setup Mobile Setup Troubleshooting

Quick Setup (All Devices)

  1. Select "Campus-Secure" from available WiFi networks
  2. Enter your student username and password
  3. Accept the security certificate when prompted
  4. You should now be connected to the internet
  5. If you experience issues, try the device-specific guides above
Student ID Card Services

Your student ID card is your key to campus life. Use it for dining, library access, building entry, and more.

Card Services

  • New Student Cards: Get your first campus ID card
  • Replacement Cards: Lost or damaged card replacement ($15 fee)
  • Photo Updates: Update your ID photo once per academic year
  • Add Flex Dollars: Load money onto your card for purchases
  • Card Activation: Activate new cards and features
Schedule ID Card Appointment Check Card Balance

What to Bring for New ID

  1. Valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, etc.)
  2. Student enrollment verification or acceptance letter
  3. Payment for card fee if applicable ($25 for new students)
