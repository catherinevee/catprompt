Request Room Change

Request a change to your current housing assignment. Whether you need a different room type, want to join friends, or need accommodations, we're here to help find the best solution for your needs.

Room Change Period

Spring Changes: Room change requests for Spring 2024 are accepted through February 15th. Changes may be subject to availability and a $75 processing fee. Emergency requests are considered year-round.

Request Processing Timeline

1
Request Submitted

Your request is received and assigned a tracking number. You'll receive email confirmation within 24 hours.

2
Initial Review

Housing staff reviews your request, documentation, and current housing status. May request additional information.

3
Room Matching

Available rooms are identified based on your preferences and requirements. Priority matching occurs.

4
Approval Decision

Request is approved or denied. If approved, you'll receive new assignment details and move instructions.

5
Move Coordination

Move-out and move-in dates are scheduled. Key transfer and final arrangements are completed.

Important Information
  • Processing Fee: $75 for non-emergency requests
  • Deadline: February 15 for Spring changes
  • Medical Requests: No fee with documentation
  • Emergency: 24-48 hour processing
  • Standard: 2-4 weeks processing
  • Appeals: 10 business days for decisions
Required Documentation
  • Medical: Healthcare provider letter
  • Accessibility: Disability services verification
  • Academic: Program coordinator letter
  • Emergency: Incident report or documentation
  • Roommate Issues: Mediation attempt records
Alternative Options
  • Roommate Mediation Services
