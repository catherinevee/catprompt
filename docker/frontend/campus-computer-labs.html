Available Software

Our computer labs provide access to a comprehensive suite of software for academic, research, and creative work.

Office & Productivity

  • Microsoft Office 365: Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook
  • Google Workspace: Docs, Sheets, Slides
  • LibreOffice: Open-source office suite
  • Adobe Acrobat Pro: PDF creation and editing
  • OneNote: Digital note-taking

Creative & Design

  • Adobe Creative Cloud: Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign
  • Adobe Premiere Pro: Video editing
  • Adobe After Effects: Motion graphics
  • Sketch: UI/UX design (Mac only)

How to Print

  1. Send documents to campus printers from any lab computer
  2. Use your student ID card at any print station
  3. Select print options and confirm payment
  4. Collect your documents immediately

Supported Formats

  • Documents: PDF, Word, PowerPoint, Excel
  • Images: JPEG, PNG, TIFF, BMP
  • Large Format: PDF, AI, PSD (up to 36" wide)
  • 3D Models: STL, OBJ, PLY files

Lab Policies & Guidelines

  • Valid student ID required for lab access
  • Maximum 4-hour session limit during peak hours
  • No food or drinks near computers
  • Save work frequently and log off when finished
  • Report technical issues to lab attendants immediately
  • Respect quiet zones in designated study areas
  • Personal software installation not permitted
  • USB drives must be virus-scanned before use
